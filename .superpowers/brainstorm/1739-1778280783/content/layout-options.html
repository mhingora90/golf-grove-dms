Sales Module — Layout Options

How should the sales module be structured in the sidebar and on screen?

A

Single nav item — tabbed inside

One "Sales" nav entry. Inside: tabs for Unit Register and Revenue Overview. Clean sidebar, everything in one place.

── Finance ──
💰 Finance Overview
📄 Payment Certs
📊 BOQ
── Sales ──
🏠 Sales
B

Two nav items — Unit Register + Revenue

Separate sidebar entries: Unit Register (the 98-unit table) and Sales Revenue (the financial overview). Mirrors the Finance/BOQ/IPC pattern already in the app.

── Sales ──
🏠 Unit Register
📈 Sales Revenue
C

Three nav items — Register, Revenue, Setup

Separate entries for Unit Register, Sales Revenue, and Unit Setup (bulk import / edit master list). Setup stays hidden once units are configured.

── Sales ──
🏠 Unit Register
📈 Sales Revenue
⚙️ Unit Setup
